Parametric Generation of Impulse Responses for Audio Effects

Riccardo Simionato, Stefano Fasciani
University of Oslo
Paper Code

Abstract

This paper introduces a novel method for modeling audio effects such as filters and reverbs by the parametric generation of their impulse responses. Experimental results demonstrate that our proposed method effectively models audio effects that conventional neural networks struggle to handle. Additionally, it achieves precise alignment with the phase response and exceeds baseline performance metrics. The generation of the impulse response is influenced by both device-specific parameters and additional variables that characterize different types of hardware audio effects. Moreover, perceptual tests affirm our objective results, highlighting the effectiveness of the proposed approach.
